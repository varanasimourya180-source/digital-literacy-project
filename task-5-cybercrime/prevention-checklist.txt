Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

General Safety Tips
Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
Do not click on suspicious links received via email, SMS, or social media
Keep your apps and devices updated with the latest security patches
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like logging into accounts
Always verify unknown contacts before sharing any personal information

Financial / UPI Safety Tips
Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank
Do not approve “collect requests” unless you are sure about the sender
Always double-check the recipient’s name before sending money
Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown or untrusted sources

Reporting Cybercrime
Report incidents on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 immediately in case of financial fraud
