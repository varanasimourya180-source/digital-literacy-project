Task 1: Infographic Description

For Task 1, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva. The aim of the infographic was to help students understand the importance of digital literacy in their academic and personal lives. The design includes key topics such as the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, and email etiquette.

In the infographic, I explained digital literacy as the ability to effectively use digital devices, communicate online, and evaluate information. I also highlighted useful tools like Google Docs, GitHub, and LinkedIn, which help students in learning, collaboration, and building a professional profile. Additionally, I included important safety practices such as using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and protecting personal information online. Email etiquette tips like using a proper subject line and maintaining a polite tone were also included.

One challenge I faced was organizing all the information in a visually appealing and simple format without overcrowding the design. Overall, this task helped me improve my creativity and understand how to present information clearly and effectively.
