Social Media Do’s and Don’ts (For College Students)

Do’s
Use a professional and respectful tone in your posts
Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings
Verify information before sharing it
Build a positive digital presence (projects, achievements, skills)
Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely

Don’ts
Don’t share personal or sensitive information (OTP, address, etc.)
Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
Don’t spread fake news or unverified information
Don’t engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
Don’t overshare details that may harm your reputation
